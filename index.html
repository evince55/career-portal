About

I'm Eugene Vincent, a second-year CS student at UIC who treats a homelab like production: version-controlled, monitored, and alerting me before my users — mostly friends on the Minecraft server — notice anything.

This site deploys to Cloudflare Pages but reads its live stats straight from the cluster.
